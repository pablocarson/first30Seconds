About this app

About this app

Welcome to First 30 Seconds! Our mission is to get you face to face with people you'd like to date.

If you've tried online dating, this might sound familiar: you see someone interesting and wonder if there could be a connection. After a few emails, you arrange a time and place, maybe a coffee shop, and in 30 seconds it becomes obvious that there's no chemistry. So you stay long enough to be polite, wrap it up, and start over again.

Research proves that chemistry matters more than anything you can learn about someone from words and pictures. So we host parties in places convenient to you where you can go to meet others. Here's how it works:

  • When you're ready, we send you to a nearby location.
  • When you arrive, we'll show you others who are there to meet someone like you.
  • Tell us how interested you are in meeting them.
  • If you're BOTH interested, we'll try to match you up, and you get to meet them on the spot.
  • When you're done, close the match, and get ready for the next match.

So after you provide a photo and some very basic preference info, you'll be ready to go. We'll give you enough credits to try a few parties for free. Good luck!
